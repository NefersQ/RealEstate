How to create a 3D model with your IPhone

You can easily create 3D models of your apartments or house using your iPhone with LiDAR scanner:

  1. Please download a LiDAR scanning app from the App Store(Polycam, 3D Scanner App etc.)
  2. Open the app and start a new scan
  3. Walk slowly around your apartment and ensure that all possible angles are covered
  4. After scanning, export the model in GLB(GLTF) format
  5. Upload model file here for potential buyers to explore in 3D
